executive summary

The Nessain Family Organization NeFOR stands to serve the purpose of administering how we feed, Educate, Empower, and keep the welfare of the Nessian household and Liberia communities through the pillars of: Agriculture, Education, Health, and Empowerment.

For this vesion we are taking on the mission of cultivating Competent, Responsible and Accountable men and women; and providing opportunities of Self-sustainment to Liberians in Liberia.
